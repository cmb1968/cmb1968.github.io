I am Director of Research in Physics and Deputy Director of the [Institute for Computational Cosmology](https://www.dur.ac.uk/icc/) at Durham University. My research covers galaxy formation modelling using the GALFORM semi-analytic model, large-scale structure and two-point clustering statistics, and the application of machine learning and emulator methods to cosmology.

Current projects include sparse-sampling corrections for galaxy clustering measurements (applied to GALFORM on the P-Millennium simulation), combining GALFORM emission-line predictions with PAU Survey narrow-band photometry, and symbolic-regression approaches to galaxy stellar mass estimation.
