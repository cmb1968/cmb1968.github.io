Carlton M. Baugh

Director of Research in Physics & Deputy Director, Institute for Computational Cosmology, Durham University

Department of Physics

Durham University

Durham, UK

I am Director of Research in Physics and Deputy Director of the Institute for Computational Cosmology at Durham University. My research covers galaxy formation modelling using the GALFORM semi-analytic model, large-scale structure and two-point clustering statistics, and the application of machine learning and emulator methods to cosmology.

Current projects include sparse-sampling corrections for galaxy clustering measurements (applied to GALFORM on the P-Millennium simulation), combining GALFORM emission-line predictions with PAU Survey narrow-band photometry, and symbolic-regression approaches to galaxy stellar mass estimation.
